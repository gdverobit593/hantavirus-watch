## No specific antiviral treatment

Currently, there is **no approved antiviral drug** or vaccine for hantavirus infection in humans.

## Supportive care

Treatment focuses on managing symptoms and supporting organ function:

- **Oxygen therapy** — for breathing difficulties
- **Fluid management** — to maintain blood pressure and kidney function
- **Dialysis** — if kidney failure occurs (HFRS)
- **ECMO** — in severe HPS cases with respiratory failure
- **Intensive care monitoring** — critical for severe cases

## Prognosis

- **HPS**: About 38% fatality rate; survival depends on early hospitalization
- **HFRS**: Generally lower fatality (1–15%); mild cases may recover fully
- **Early medical attention** dramatically improves outcomes

## What to do if exposed

If you think you were exposed to rodents and develop flu-like symptoms:

1. **Seek medical attention immediately**
2. **Tell your doctor about the rodent exposure**
3. **Monitor symptoms** closely for breathing difficulties
